Thomas Doerksen

About Thomas

Thomas is a poet, scientist, and philosopher. He has a PhD in Theory and Criticism from Western University, a Masters in philosophy from Guelph University, a Masters in synthetic organic chemistry from Victoria University, and a Bachelors of chemistry from the King's University in Edmonton.
